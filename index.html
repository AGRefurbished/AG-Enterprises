Our Story

Welcome to A&G Enterprises, where every piece has a story and a second chance.

It all started in our home garage, where we have been refurbishing furniture and tools for years—just for our household and close friends. Over time, we realized how much we love restoring forgotten pieces, bringing them back to life with care and craftsmanship.

After selling our first refurbished item, we saw the excitement and appreciation from the customer who valued high-quality, repurposed work. That’s when we decided to share our ability with others who have the same passion for unique, well-crafted pieces.

Every item we restore is given a fresh start, with attention to detail and a commitment to quality. Whether it is a vintage tool with a rich history or a furniture piece that just needs new life, our goal is to ensure it finds the right home.

Thank you for supporting small craftsmanship. We hope you find something here that speaks to you.
